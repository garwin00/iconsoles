Nintendo_

Founded in 1889 by Fusajiro Yamauchi as Nintendo Koppai selling handmade playing cards, Nintendo is one of the most influential gaming companies today. the company evolved from playing cards to toys and eventually to video gaming. To date, they've released seven consoles, starting with the Nintendo Entertainment System in 1983. Most recenetly, they've released the Switch, their most powerful and fastest selling console to date.

Sony_

In 1946 Masaru Ibuka and Akio Morita, two electronic industrialists, founded Tokyo Tsushin Kogyo KK to sell electronics. In 1956 the company was renamed Sonī Kabushiki Kaisha, and is now just referred to as Sony. Today they're one of the leading electronic manufacturers in the world. To date, they've released six consoles, starting with the Playstation in 1994. Today, we have the PS4 Pro, their third iteration of the dominant PS4.

xbox_

Xbox is a gaming brand and subdivison of Microsoft introduced in 2001 with the Xbox console. This was Microsoft's first foray into console manufacturing and was released as a competitor to Sony's Playstation 2. The Xbox was the first american console to be released in seven years after Atari's jaguar stopped production in 1996. Today, the xbox is the main competitor to Sony's Playstation and Microsoft have released 3 major consoles, again with several iterations on each, the most recent being the Xbox One X, the current most powerful console on the market.
